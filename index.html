How to play

The goal of this game is to keep Hyper Games in business for 5 years. We did it, how hard can it be?

Respond to the cards by swiping left or right.

Keep an eye on these indicators. If they max out or goes to zero you will lose the game.

Protip: A white dot will appear over the parameter(s) that will be affected by your choice.

Good luck!
